Temporal Anti-Aliasing is a post effect solution to reduce or remove the effects of temporal aliasing for Unity.

Temporal Anti-Aliasing can get a better quality than other ' spatial only ' solutions such as SSAA, MSAA, FXAA, SMAA, MLAA and many others. Performance of Temporal AA is approximately equivalent to FXAA3 and scalable for PC/Mac/Linux/Web/Mobile platforms. It works fine both with forward and deferred rendering path.
